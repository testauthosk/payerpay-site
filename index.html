PayerPay helps individuals, entrepreneurs and companies manage international payments in one platform. Users can obtain USD and EUR payment details, send and receive transfers worldwide, exchange currencies and use supported cryptocurrencies.

Payments can be sent to friends, relatives, customers, suppliers and business partners. Services are provided directly by PayerPay and through regulated banking and payment partners in the United States and the European Union.

Individuals aged 18 or older from most countries may apply. US or EU residency is not required to obtain USD or EUR payment details.

Accounts are unavailable in sanctioned and unsupported jurisdictions.

Register, complete identity verification and apply for USD or EUR payment details. Once approved, the details are issued in your verified name and displayed in your account.

USD details support ACH and Fedwire. EUR details support SEPA Instant. There is no opening or monthly maintenance fee.

You can add money by bank transfer to your USD or EUR details or by depositing supported cryptocurrency.

Funds can be sent worldwide through bank transfers, SWIFT and local methods such as UPI, PIX, GCash and FPS. Fees, rates and processing times are shown before confirmation.

Yes. PayerPay is registered with FINTRAC and the Bank of Canada.

Payments are protected by encryption and mandatory two-factor authentication. Customer funds are held with licensed international banks. PayerPay also performs identity verification, transaction monitoring, sanctions screening and checks of recipients and external cryptocurrency addresses.
